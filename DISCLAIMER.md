# Medical & Content Disclaimer

This site is an educational reference. It is not a substitute for individualized medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

- Content reflects evidence available as of mid-2026 (see each page's References section for source dates and tiers). Evidence in dermatology and aesthetic medicine evolves; treat any specific claim as current-to-the-best-of-research-at-writing-time, not permanently settled.
- The **Cosmetic Procedures** page carries an elevated, page-top disclaimer (consistent with how the rest of the site is built) because it discusses injectables, devices, and surgery performed by licensed professionals. Nothing on that page, or anywhere else on this site, substitutes for an in-person consultation with a qualified provider.
- The **Hyperpigmentation** page includes a distinct medical-signal callout (acanthosis nigricans) flagging when a cosmetic-seeming concern may warrant evaluation by a physician for an underlying metabolic cause. This is a general educational pattern-recognition aid, not a diagnostic tool.
- Pricing on the **Products Guide** page is directional and will drift over time; it is not a live price feed.
- Where a cited study was industry-funded, that funding relationship is disclosed directly in the relevant page's References section, consistent with this project's evidence-standards framework (see `/docs/Phase_2_Research_Standards.md`).

If you are experiencing a medical concern, please consult a licensed healthcare provider rather than relying on this site.
